Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: While both Merging and Rebasing integrate changes from one branch into 
another, resulting in the same final code, they handle this differently. Merging 
preserves the full branching history while Rebasing rewrites the history for a 
cleaner, linear history.
